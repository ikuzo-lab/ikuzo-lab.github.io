About Us

Our laboratory adopts the Japanese kanji 「異」 meaning "different" or "alternate". This character is commonly found in 「異世界」or "Isekai" which commonly refer to specific genre of Japanese art (anime, game, novel, or comic) to depict an alternate reality of while keeping all the sense of reality intact. Our laboratory focuses on realizing the concept of Isekai by conducting research on Cross-Reality system (XR) that encompass Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and Mixed reality (MR). Our focus is to remove the boundary between reality and virtual through multimodal interaction to make the alternate reality closer.
